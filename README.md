# 🏜️ Can I Build an App for That?
### The OCI Hack-at-SKO Build-Off — Las Vegas

> **70 teams. One question. A whole lot of creative chaos.**

---

## 🎯 Overview

Welcome to the main event. At **SKO (Sales Kickoff) in Las Vegas**, **70 pre-assigned teams** of OCI **sellers and engineers** go head-to-head, racing the clock to answer the only question that matters this week:

> ## _"Can I build an app for that?"_

Spoiler: the answer is almost always **yes** — but you've only got until the buzzer to prove it. Sellers bring the vision, engineers bring the build, and together each team turns a napkin sketch into a living, breathing demo before time runs out. No idea is too wild, no timeline too tight. That's the whole point.

---

## 🚀 What to Expect

| | |
|---|---|
| 🎤 **Live demos** | Real apps, built in real time, presented live — no slideware, no smoke and mirrors. |
| 🧠 **Engineering mentors** | Stuck? Roaming OCI experts are on hand to unblock you, pair on the hard parts, and keep you moving. |
| 🏗️ **Creative chaos** | Whiteboards, half-finished ideas, last-minute pivots, and the glorious scramble of a room full of builders. |
| 🏆 **Healthy competition** | Bragging rights are on the line. Bring your A-game (and maybe a backup plan). |

---

## 🥇 The Main Stage

When the dust settles, the **top 3 teams** earn the spotlight: a live presentation on the **main stage Thursday morning**, in front of the entire crowd.

And the winner? **You** decide — the champion is crowned by **live audience vote**. 🗳️

🤑 Every member of the winning team walks away with a **$250 prize**. Glory _and_ cash.
